3.3 Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects – what would you do differently next time? Teamwork? Use of Slack?

Bringing teammates near and far closer together with communication software. Effective communication is crucial for team productivity. Using Slack is vital if your team is challenged by geography. Cloud-based project or task management software such as Basecamp is great for keeping your team on track and on the right page.

3.4 Describe how you would go about testing digital solutions

Testing digital solutions should be baked in part of the process of any project. Even during the nebulous design phase error checking should be ongoing. Once the team are happy with the final build then a rigorous in house or unit quality assurance pass should take place. If the project is web-based then testing on a wide spectrum of internet browsers such as chrome, firefox, safari and opera should be a prerequisite. Once the internal testing has been completed then an open beta test to a select number of outside trusted users should be carried out. Feedback taken from open social media channels should be acted on before a final public release date is set or announced.
